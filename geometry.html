triangle properties

sum of interior angles =
180°
sum of exterior angles =
360°
area =
½ × base × height
pythagoras theorem =
a² + b² = c²
semi-perimeter (s) =
(a + b + c)/2
heron’s formula =
√[s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)]

circle properties

circumference =
2πr
area =
πr²
diameter =
2r
arc length (θ in radians) =
rθ
sector area (θ in radians) =
½r²θ
chord length =
2r sin(θ/2)

quadrilateral properties

sum of interior angles =
360°
area of square =
side²
area of rectangle =
length × breadth
area of parallelogram =
base × height
area of rhombus =
(diagonal₁ × diagonal₂)/2
area of trapezium =
½ × (sum of parallel sides) × height

polygon properties

sum of interior angles =
(n - 2) × 180°
sum of exterior angles =
360°
number of diagonals =
n(n - 3)/2
area of regular polygon =
(1/2) × Perimeter × Apothem

measure of one interior angle (regular) =
[(n - 2) × 180°] / n
measure of one exterior angle (regular) =
360° / n
